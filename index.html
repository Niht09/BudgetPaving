Questions people ask before hiring a paving contractor.

Asphalt paving cost depends on the project size, base condition, access, grading, thickness, repair needs, and whether the job is a driveway, parking lot, acreage lane, or resurfacing project. The best way to price it accurately is with a site review and free estimate.
Small cracks, potholes, and isolated low spots may be repairable. If the surface is worn but the base is still stable, resurfacing or overlay may work. If the base is failing, full replacement may be the better long-term option.
